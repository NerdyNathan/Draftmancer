Welcome to MTGADraft!

Want to draft in MTGA with your friends for free? Good!

Use the controls on the top of the page to enter your User Name and indicate your preferences. Upload your MTGA logs (you should find them at C:\Users\YourName\AppData\LocalLow\Wizards Of The Coast\MTGA\output_log.txt) to restrict the draft to the cards you actually own in the game. Share your session ID with your friends (or join their own session) and click ready to start drafting!

Options:

  • Session ID: A unique identifier for your session, just make sure to use the same as your friends to play with them!
  • Boosters per Player: Shared by everyone in your session.
  • Restrict Set: Select a set to draft using only with cards from this set. Shared by everyone in your session.
  • Restrict to Collection: If unchecked, your collection will not limit the cards available. If every players unchecks this, you will draft using every cards. (Particularly useful if you don't intend on playing specifically on MTGA)
