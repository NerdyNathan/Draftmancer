MTGADraft Cube Format

Basic cube list is one English card name per line, just like .txt export in either Cube Cobra or Cube Tutor. Boosters will be 15 cards without regards for rarity distribution.

If you want more control over your booster, like a custom 'rarity' distribution, you can use the following format: 

			
			[SlotName(CardPerBooster)]
			Card 1
			Card 2
			...
			[Slot2Name(CardPerBooster)]
			Card X
			Card X+1
			...
Each slot will be present in each booster. For example: 
			
			[Common(10)]
			Card 1
			Card 2
			...
			[Uncommon(3)]
			Card X
			Card X+1
			...
			[Rare(1)]
			Card Y
			Card Y+1
			...
Will mimic the distribution of a standard Magic booster, except for the 1/8 Mythic/Rare ratio. Cards in the 'Common' slot don't have to be 'real' commons!

Why not add a special planeswalker slot in each of your booster instead of one of the commons, everyone loved them in WAR right? :D 

			
			[Planewalker(1)]
			Planewalker 1
			Planewalker 2
			...
			[Common(9)]
			Card 1

			...
